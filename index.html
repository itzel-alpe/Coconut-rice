Welcome to our INTERNATIONAL HIGH SKILL where we will embark on a culinary journey to explore the world of measurements, units, and ingredients. Through this hands-on experience, you will not only improve your English language proficiency but also gain practical knowledge in the kitchen. Get ready for an engaging and flavorful learning experience that combines language acquisition with the joy of cooking!
